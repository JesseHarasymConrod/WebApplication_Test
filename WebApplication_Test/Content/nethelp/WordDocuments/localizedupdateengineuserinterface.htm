Localized Update Engine User Interface

The AppLife Update engine has been localized for the following cultures:

•      English (en-us) – default

•      Chinese – Traditional (zh-CHT)

•      French (fr)

•      German (de)

•      Italian (it)

•      Japanese (ja)

•      Spanish (es)

 

When creating an update project, you can choose one or more languages to provide your update for.  You can also supply culture specific release notes for each language selected.

 

Customers who would like the AppLife Update Engine localized to their culture and are willing to perform the string translations can find information about this process at http://www.kineticjump.com/update/localization.aspx
