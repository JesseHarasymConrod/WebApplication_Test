License Form

The license form allows you to enter your purchased license number.  AppLife Update will operate in trial mode for 30 days after installation, after which the build functions will cease to operate until the software is properly licensed.

 

Your current license number is displayed on this form.

 

You can purchase a license by clicking on the “Get a license number” button.

 

To enter or replace a license number, type or paste your license number into the New license number text box and click OK.

 

See License Management for more information..
